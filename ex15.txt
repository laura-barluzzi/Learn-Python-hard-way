Banana bread: 

Ingredients: 
- 3 bananas
- 2 beaten eggs
- a bit of oil/butter/milk/yogurt
- 15 tablespoons of sugar
- 22 tablespoons of flour
- a pintch of salt
- a tablespoon of baking powerd

Process: 
1) One bowl mix: flour, sugar, salt and baking powder
2) One bowl mix the liquids: beaten eggs, the oil/butter/etc.
3) Finely chopped/smash bananas and mix them in the bowl with liquid. 
4) Add the dry bowl into the liquid, stir. 
5) Put in the loaf tray and in the oven (350Â°) for 1 hour.
